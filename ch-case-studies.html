    Chapter 7 Case Studies

    The sections in this chapter are a guide for preparing each of your case study presentations. Your case study presentation will be scored out of \(30\) points, assessed according to the following rubric. Every category will be scored the same for your whole group, with the exception of the Peer Review category. Please use it as a reference while preparing for your presentation.
